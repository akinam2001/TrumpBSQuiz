About

Donald J. Trump is known to be a massive liar while others see him as the ultimate speaker of truth. Those that perceive him as a liar see through the bullshit by fact checking him and questioning the credibility of his sources of information. On the other hand, those that see him as the ultimate speaker of truth take his words as gospel even if the claims Trump makes are ridiculous. In order to understand how Trump amassed a huge following through his rhetoric, it is important to explore his appeal towards his followers and what need he fulfills so we can prevent more Trump-like figures from gaining credibility in American politics. The website guides you through his history of speech, his political ideology, and the common traits seen in his followers and culminates with a quiz.
